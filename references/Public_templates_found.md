# Public templates identified (web search)

This skill includes **original** Excel RFQ datasheet templates (in `assets/`).
This reference list documents **publicly accessible** forms and specifications that were reviewed to determine what fields are typically required to obtain priceable quotes.

## Screening equipment

1. **Equipwater Automatic Wastewater Screening Equipment Catalog (PDF)**
   - URL: https://www.equipwater.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/catalogue-equipwater-screening-equipment-english.pdf
   - Why it matters: covers various screening equipment types including bar screens, vertical screens, rotary drums, and screenings handling.

2. **WEF Liquid Stream Fundamentals: Screening (PDF)**
   - URL: https://www.wef.org/globalassets/assets-wef/direct-download-library/public/03---resources/wsec-2017-fs-020-mrrdc-lsf-screening_final.pdf
   - Why it matters: technical fundamentals for screening equipment selection and design criteria.

3. **FB Procédés Wastewater Screens Brochure (PDF)**
   - URL: https://www.fb-procedes.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Brochure-FB-Procédés-1.pdf
   - Why it matters: covers wastewater screens, screw conveyors, and screenings handling equipment.
