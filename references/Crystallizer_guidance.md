# Crystallizer System datasheet guidance

Covers: 510-FC, 520-ATFD

## What to decide up front

**1) Crystallizer type**
- **Forced Circulation (FC)**: most common for ZLD, mixed suspension
- **Draft Tube Baffle (DTB)**: better crystal size control
- **Oslo-type**: classified bed, large crystals
- **Fluidized bed**: specialty applications

**2) Product requirements**
- **Crystal form**: anhydrous vs hydrated salts
- **Crystal size**: affects dewatering and handling
- **Purity requirements**: washing, recrystallization
- **Single salt vs mixed salts**: separation needed?

**3) Solids handling**
- **Centrifuge**: for crystal dewatering
- **Belt filter**: alternative dewatering
- **Dryer**: for dry product (ATFD, rotary, fluid bed)
- **Mixed salt cake**: landfill disposal

## Process data that drives the quote

### Feed characteristics
- Feed flow rate (m³/h)
- Feed TDS (typically 15-30% for crystallizer feed)
- Temperature
- Complete ionic composition
- Scaling/fouling constituents

### Crystallization parameters
- Operating temperature
- Residence time: 1-4 hours typical
- Magma density: 20-40% solids
- Crystal production rate (kg/h)

### Heat transfer
- Heat input (for evaporation)
- Steam pressure or hot oil temperature
- Boiling point elevation at operating concentration
- Heat exchanger surface area

### Solids characteristics
- Crystal size distribution target
- Crystal morphology
- Slurry handling properties

### For ATFD (Agitated Thin Film Dryer)
- Feed rate (m³/h or kg/h)
- Feed solids content
- Product moisture target
- Heating medium temperature
- Vapor handling

## Mechanical / design interface items

- **Crystallizer body**: large vessel, agitator, draft tube
- **Circulation pump**: high solids handling, abrasion resistant
- **Heat exchanger**: forced circulation type, scaling service
- **Vapor handling**: condenser, vacuum system
- **Crystal removal**: classifying leg, elutriation
- **Centrifuge/filter**: solids dewatering
- **Dryer**: final moisture removal

## Materials considerations

- Highly corrosive environment (chlorides, sulfates)
- Super duplex, Hastelloy, or titanium may be required
- Rubber or polymer linings for pumps
- Erosion from crystal slurry

## What to attach with the datasheet

- Complete feed analysis with all major ions
- Solubility data for expected salts
- Product specifications (moisture, size, purity)
- Steam/utilities availability
- Solids disposal requirements
