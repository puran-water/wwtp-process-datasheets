# Tanks & Vessels datasheet guidance

Covers: TK-POLY (Polyethylene), TK-FRP (FRP), TK-SS (Stainless Steel), TK-BOLT (Bolted Steel), TK-API (API 650), V-ASME (ASME Vessel), V-FRP (FRP Vessel), V-SS (SS Vessel)

## What to decide up front

**1) Material selection**

| Service | Recommended Material |
|---------|---------------------|
| Chemical storage (acids) | HDPE (TK-POLY), FRP (TK-FRP) |
| Chemical storage (caustic) | HDPE, FRP, or SS |
| Polymer storage | HDPE, SS |
| Diesel/fuel | Steel (TK-API) |
| Process water | Bolted steel (TK-BOLT), FRP |
| Pressure service | ASME vessel (V-ASME), SS (V-SS) |
| GAC/IX contactors | FRP vessel (V-FRP), rubber-lined |

**2) Construction type**
- **Shop-fabricated**: smaller vessels, shipped complete
- **Field-erected**: large tanks, assembled on-site
- **Bolted panel**: field-assembled, relocatable

**3) Tank roof type (atmospheric)**
- **Cone roof (fixed)**: standard
- **Dome roof (geodesic)**: clear span, aluminum
- **Floating roof**: for volatile products
- **Open top**: process tanks

**4) Design code**
- **API 650**: atmospheric storage tanks
- **API 620**: low-pressure storage
- **ASME Section VIII**: pressure vessels
- **AWWA D100/D103**: welded/bolted water tanks

## Process data that drives the quote

### Sizing
- Working volume (m³ or gallons)
- Dimensions: diameter × height or L × W × H
- Freeboard requirements
- Maximum operating level

### Design conditions
- Design pressure: atmospheric, low-pressure, or MAWP
- Design temperature range
- Vacuum rating (if applicable)
- Specific gravity of contents

### Contents
- Fluid stored
- Chemical concentration
- Temperature
- Corrosivity
- Solids content

### Site conditions
- Seismic zone
- Wind loading (per ASCE 7)
- Snow load (for roofs)
- Frost depth (for foundations)

## Technology-specific guidance

### Polyethylene Tanks (TK-POLY)
- Rotomolded (seamless) or welded
- UV stabilized for outdoor
- Max temperature: typically 50-60°C
- Fitting options: flanged or threaded

### FRP Tanks (TK-FRP)
- Resin type: isophthalic, vinyl ester, epoxy
- Laminate schedule for structural design
- Liner for chemical resistance
- Temperature limit depends on resin

### Bolted Steel Tanks (TK-BOLT)
- Glass-fused-to-steel or epoxy-coated
- Panel sizes and bolt patterns
- Gasket material for joints
- Foundation requirements

### API 650 Tanks (TK-API)
- Shell courses and plate thickness
- Roof type and support
- Nozzle schedule
- Appurtenances (vents, access, gauging)

### ASME Pressure Vessels (V-ASME)
- Design code and edition
- MAWP and MDMT
- Hydrostatic test pressure
- Nozzle schedule and orientation
- Code stamping and documentation

## Mechanical / design interface items

- **Foundation**: ringwall, slab, or pile-supported
- **Nozzles**: inlet, outlet, overflow, vent, drain
- **Appurtenances**: level instruments, access, ladders
- **Secondary containment**: dike, liner, double-wall
- **Venting**: pressure/vacuum vent, conservation vent
- **Heating/cooling**: coils, jackets, external panels

## What to attach with the datasheet

- Chemical compatibility data
- Site geotechnical report
- Foundation drawings
- P&ID showing tank connections
- Containment requirements (regulatory)
