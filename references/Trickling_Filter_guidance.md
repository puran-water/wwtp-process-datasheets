# Trickling Filter datasheet guidance

Covers: 260-TF, 260-TFD

## What to decide up front

**1) Filter application**
- **Roughing filter**: high-rate, BOD reduction only, followed by secondary treatment
- **Secondary treatment**: complete BOD removal to <30 mg/L
- **Nitrifying filter**: tertiary nitrification, low organic loading
- **Combined carbon/nitrification**: two-stage systems

**2) Media type**
- **Rock/slag**: traditional, heavy, limited depth (1.5-2.5m), low cost
- **Plastic (random)**: lightweight, higher depth possible (4-12m)
- **Plastic (structured/cross-flow)**: highest surface area, best ventilation
- **Plastic (vertical flow)**: high hydraulic loading

**3) Flow distribution**
- **Rotary distributor**: most common, self-propelled or motor-driven
- **Fixed nozzles**: for small installations

## Process data that drives the quote

### Hydraulic loading
- Design average flow (m³/h)
- Design peak flow (m³/h)
- Recirculation ratio: 0.5-4.0 typical
- Hydraulic loading: 10-40 m³/m²/d (low-rate) to 80-200 m³/m²/d (high-rate)

### Organic loading
- Influent BOD (mg/L)
- BOD loading: 0.1-0.4 kg BOD/m³/d (low-rate), 0.5-2.0 kg BOD/m³/d (high-rate)
- Effluent BOD target (mg/L)

### Media specifications
- Specific surface area: 80-200 m²/m³ typical for plastic
- Void ratio: >90% for plastic media
- Media depth: 4-6 m typical for plastic
- Tower diameter or dimensions

### Ventilation
- Natural draft (most common)
- Forced ventilation (for deep towers or poor ambient conditions)

## Mechanical / design interface items

- **Distributor arm**: speed control, bearing design, seal type
- **Underdrain system**: structural support, ventilation, effluent collection
- **Recirculation pumps**: sized for recirculation ratio
- **Humus tank**: secondary clarifier for solids separation
- **Ventilation**: louvers, fans, odor control

## Operations considerations

- Distributor maintenance and seal replacement
- Media fouling/plugging monitoring
- Snail/filter fly control
- Cold weather operation (icing potential)

## What to attach with the datasheet

- Process design calculations
- Site plan showing filter and clarifier locations
- Structural considerations (tower loads)
- Odor control requirements
