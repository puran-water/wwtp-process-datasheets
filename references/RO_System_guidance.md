# RO (Reverse Osmosis) System datasheet guidance

Covers: 350-ROS (skid), 350-ROM (membrane), 350-ROV (pressure vessel)

## What to decide up front

**1) Application**
- **Brackish water desalination**: TDS 1,000-10,000 mg/L
- **Seawater desalination**: TDS >30,000 mg/L
- **Wastewater reuse**: tertiary effluent treatment
- **Industrial process water**: high-purity production
- **ZLD concentrate treatment**: high-recovery systems

**2) Configuration**
- **Single-pass**: standard, 70-85% recovery
- **Two-pass**: for high-purity permeate (boiler feed, electronics)
- **Concentrate staging**: multiple stages for high recovery
- **Permeate staging**: multiple passes for quality

**3) Membrane type**
- **Spiral wound**: standard, most cost-effective
- **DTRO (Disc Tube)**: for high-fouling, high-TDS
- **Tubular**: extreme fouling applications

**4) Energy recovery (for high-pressure systems)**
- **Pressure exchanger**: highest efficiency (95%+)
- **Turbocharger**: moderate efficiency
- **None**: for low-pressure BWRO

## Process data that drives the quote

### Hydraulic design
- Design feed flow (m³/h)
- Design permeate flow (m³/h)
- Recovery: 50-85% BWRO, 35-50% SWRO
- Concentrate disposal capacity

### Feed water quality
- TDS and ionic composition
- SDI (Silt Density Index): must be <5, preferably <3
- Turbidity: <1 NTU
- TOC, color, organics
- Temperature range
- Silica, barium, strontium (scaling potential)
- Biological activity

### Membrane performance
- Flux: 15-25 LMH (BWRO), 10-15 LMH (SWRO)
- Salt rejection: 99-99.8%
- Permeate TDS target
- Number of elements and vessels

### Operating pressures
- BWRO: 1.0-2.5 MPa (150-360 psi)
- SWRO: 5.5-8.0 MPa (800-1160 psi)
- Pressure drop allowance

### Chemical requirements
- Antiscalant type and dose
- pH adjustment (acid)
- Chlorine/dechlorination
- CIP chemicals

## Mechanical / design interface items

- **High-pressure pumps**: multistage centrifugal or positive displacement
- **Pressure vessels**: FRP, 4" or 8" diameter standard
- **Membrane elements**: 4040 or 8040 standard sizes
- **Piping and valves**: high-pressure rated, duplex SS for SWRO
- **CIP system**: tank, pump, heating, connections
- **Instrumentation**: flow, pressure, conductivity, pH

## Pretreatment requirements

- Multimedia filtration or UF (SDI reduction)
- Cartridge filters: 5 μm before RO
- Antiscalant dosing
- Dechlorination (sodium bisulfite or GAC)

## What to attach with the datasheet

- Complete feed water analysis
- Membrane projection software output
- Site layout and space constraints
- Concentrate disposal plan
- Electrical load requirements
