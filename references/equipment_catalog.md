## Package vs Component

**Package templates** define complete systems:
- `101-SC`, `101-GR` (headworks packages)
- `230-AT`, `280-SBR` (biological treatment packages)
- `310-DMF`, `320-PUF`, `350-ROS` (filtration/membrane packages)
- `401-CL`, `401-O3`, `420-UV` (disinfection packages)
- `501-MEE`, `501-MVR`, `510-FC` (brine packages)
- `640-DIG` (digestion package)

**Component templates** define individual equipment:
- All universal equipment (PP-*, BL-*, MX-*, TK-*, V-*, HX-*, FL-*)
- Mechanisms (130-CM, 240-CM, 601-TM)
- Reactors/media (201-FFR, 250-MBR, 260-TF, 310-FV, 350-ROM)

Use `scripts/generate_bom.py` to expand a package template into its component datasheets.
