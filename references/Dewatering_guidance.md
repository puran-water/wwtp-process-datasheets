# Dewatering Equipment datasheet guidance

Covers: 610-BFP (Belt Filter Press), 610-CFG (Centrifuge), 610-FP (Filter Press), 610-VP (Volute Press)

## What to decide up front

**1) Dewatering technology**
- **Belt filter press**: 15-25% cake, moderate power, common for municipal
- **Centrifuge (decanter)**: 20-35% cake, high power, enclosed, high capacity
- **Filter press (plate & frame)**: 30-45% cake, batch operation, highest dryness
- **Volute/screw press**: 15-25% cake, low power, simple, continuous

**2) Sludge type**
- **Primary sludge**: easier to dewater, 4-8% feed
- **WAS (Waste Activated Sludge)**: harder to dewater, 0.5-2% feed
- **Mixed sludge**: intermediate, blend of primary + WAS
- **Digested sludge**: requires different conditioning

**3) Polymer conditioning**
- **Cationic polymer**: most common for municipal sludge
- **Anionic polymer**: some industrial applications
- **Dual polymer**: cationic + anionic for difficult sludges
- **Inorganic conditioning**: lime, ferric chloride (for filter press)

## Process data that drives the quote

### Sludge characteristics
- Sludge type and source
- Feed flow rate (m³/h)
- Feed solids concentration (%)
- Volatile solids content (%)
- CST (Capillary Suction Time) or specific resistance
- Temperature

### Performance targets
- Cake solids target (%)
- Solids capture: typically 95-99%
- Centrate/filtrate TSS limit

### Polymer consumption
- Active polymer dose: 2-10 kg/dry tonne (municipal)
- Polymer type and form (emulsion, dry, solution)
- Polymer makedown system

### Equipment sizing
- Dry solids throughput (kg DS/h)
- Belt width (BFP): 1.0-3.0 m
- Bowl diameter (centrifuge): 350-1000 mm
- Plate size (filter press): 800-2000 mm
- Number of units and redundancy

## Technology-specific considerations

### Belt Filter Press
- Belt type: woven or spiral
- Belt speed and tension
- Wash water requirements: 50-100 L/min per meter width
- Gravity drainage zone length

### Centrifuge
- G-force: 2000-4000 G typical
- Differential speed (scroll vs bowl)
- Pond depth adjustment
- Power consumption: 30-50 kWh/dry tonne

### Filter Press
- Cycle time: 2-4 hours per batch
- Plate opening mechanism
- Core blow and wash options
- Cake discharge method

### Volute Press
- Screw pitch progression
- Ring spacing
- Dewatering zone length

## Mechanical / design interface items

- **Polymer system**: storage, makedown, aging, metering
- **Sludge feed pumps**: progressive cavity most common
- **Conveyor/transport**: cake discharge to truck or storage
- **Odor control**: covers, ventilation
- **Wash water system**: pressure, flow, treatment

## What to attach with the datasheet

- Sludge characterization (solids, volatile, CST)
- Jar test or pilot test results
- Cake disposal requirements (landfill, incineration, land application)
- Truck loading / conveyance requirements
- Available utilities (water, power, air)
